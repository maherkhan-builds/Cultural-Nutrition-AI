# ✨ Overview

**Cultural Nutrition AI** is a culturally intelligent nutrition app that creates **personalized diet plans** aligned with a user’s:

- 🌎 regional ingredients  
- 🕌 religious dietary rules  
- 👪 family traditions  
- 🥘 cultural cuisine preferences  
- 🎯 fitness and health goals  

It keeps meal planning **realistic, sustainable, and identity-respecting** — without generic Western-centric assumptions.

---

# 🏭 Industry

Nutrition / AI Personalization

---

# 🎯 Target Audience

- Global users from diverse cultural backgrounds  
- People with religious dietary considerations  
- Families who want meal plans that respect traditions  
- Users managing goals like weight loss, muscle gain, balanced health, and medical diets  

---

# 🚨 The Core Problem

Most AI diet apps ignore:

- cultural food traditions  
- religious dietary practices  
- regional eating habits  
- availability of local ingredients  

This makes meal plans feel **unrealistic, culturally disconnected, and unsustainable** — causing low adherence and frustration.

---

# ✅ The Solution

**Cultural Nutrition AI** generates weekly meal plans that:

- use familiar dishes and ingredients  
- propose healthier versions **without removing cultural identity**  
- adapt for religious/ethical dietary rules  
- provide macro + calorie tracking with practical guidance  

It turns nutrition planning into a **culture-first health system**.

---

# 🧠 Core Features

## 1️⃣ Cultural Profile Setup
- Select country/region  
- Choose cultural cuisine preferences  
- Religious dietary considerations (optional)  
- Vegetarian / Non-vegetarian / Vegan  
- Food allergies and restrictions  
- Health goals (weight loss, muscle gain, balanced health, medical diet)  

## 2️⃣ AI Meal Plan Generator
- Culturally aligned weekly meal plan  
- Traditional dishes + familiar ingredients  
- Healthier cooking alternatives (culture-preserving)  
- Portion recommendations  
- Nutrition breakdown (calories, protein, carbs, fats)  

## 3️⃣ Smart Ingredient Intelligence
- Substitutions that preserve taste + cultural authenticity  
- Seasonal ingredient adaptation  
- Grocery list generator based on local markets  
- Budget-friendly meal options  

## 4️⃣ Nutrition Insight Dashboard
- Daily calorie balance  
- Macros distribution chart  
- Weekly progress tracker  
- Cultural Diversity Score (how well the plan respects preferences)  
- Hydration tracker  

## 5️⃣ Adaptive Learning Engine
- Adjust plans based on feedback  
- Learn favorite dishes  
- Detect non-adherence patterns  
- Recommend culturally relevant alternatives

# 🥘 Example Output (Culturally Respectful Plan)

- Lunch: Rajma bowl with brown rice + cucumber salad

# 🧮 Nutrition Logic (Simplified)

## Macro Targets
- Protein goal adjusts based on weight + goal (cut / bulk / maintain)  
- Calories calculated from activity level + goal  
- Portions scale based on adherence feedback  

## Cultural Diversity Score
Measures alignment with:
- ingredients familiar to region  
- traditional dish inclusion  
- religious rule compliance  
- substitution authenticity  
- user “comfort food” preferences
